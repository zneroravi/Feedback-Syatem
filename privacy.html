Privacy And Policy

Welcome to JustSAY. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Student and Teacher Feedback Portal.


1. Information We Collect


We collect the following types of information:


Personal Information: Names, email addresses, contact details, and other identifiers provided by students, teachers, and administrators. Feedback Data: Comments, ratings, and responses submitted through the Portal. Usage Data: IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and other technical data collected automatically when users interact with the Portal. Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic.


2. How We Use Your Information


We use the collected information for the following purposes:


To provide and improve the Portal‚Äôs functionality. To facilitate communication between students and teachers. To analyze feedback data and generate reports for institutional improvement. To ensure security and prevent unauthorized access. To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.


3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information


We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share information under the following circumstances:


With Administrators: Feedback data may be shared with educational institutions for evaluation purposes. With Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting with Portal operations, such as hosting and data analytics. Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or users.


4. Data Retention and Security


We retain collected data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Users should maintain confidentiality of their login credentials.


5. Your Rights and Choices


Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:


Access, correct, or delete your personal data. Withdraw consent for certain data processing activities. Opt-out of non-essential cookies and tracking technologies. For any data requests, please contact Us.


6. Third-Party Links and Services


Our Portal may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage users to review their policies.


7. Children's Privacy


Our Portal is intended for students and teachers. If we discover that a minor has provided personal data without parental consent, we will take appropriate action to remove the information.


8. Updates to This Privacy Policy


We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Portal after updates constitutes acceptance of the changes.
